GrocerReady

Our Project

We started GrocerReady in order to simplify the busy live of real people. GrocerReady helps you plan out all the groceries you need for your meals. Simply upload your favourite recipes, and we'll give you your shopping list! By participating in GrocerReady, you can reduce the amount of excess food purchased, and take a step towards zero food waste.

How to use

  1. Open up the GrocerReady app.

  2. Copy and paste the URLS to the recipes you would like to make.

  3. Hit enter to have your recipes assembled into a list.

  4. Once all your recipes have been included, hit submit.

  5. Enjoy your printable list of ingredients, we hope you found our app helpful! :-)
